LélekBúvár is a mental health companion focused on recording negative automatic thoughts and logical errors.

The app is built around a cognitive-behavioral idea: our thinking patterns strongly influence our moods, emotions and actions. By recording thoughts and identifying distortions, users can make those patterns easier to recognize and work with.

Focus areas:
* Recording negative automatic thoughts
* Identifying logical errors and distortions
* Supporting reflection through structured entries
* Making recurring thinking patterns more visible

The goal is to give users a simple, private tool for turning vague mental loops into something concrete enough to examine.
