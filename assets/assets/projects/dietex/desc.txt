DietEx is an all-in-one health tracking app focused on weight, diet progress and everyday motivation.

The product is built around a simple idea: progress becomes easier to manage when users can record their data consistently and understand their changes through clear statistics.

Main features:
* Straightforward, pleasant user experience
* Multiple color themes, including dark mode
* Imperial and metric unit support
* Cloud backup and restore across devices
* Daily weight tracking
* Mood and sport activity notes
* Hip, neck and waist measurements for body fat statistics
* Clear charts for long-term changes
* Configurable statistics from any chosen start date
* Starting, current and forecast BMI values
* Starting and current body fat percentage
* Weight forecast and progress percentage
* 7, 14 and 30 day trend summaries
* Daily average weight difference
* Reached and remaining goals
* Detailed weight journal

DietEx turns daily health logging into a more structured, visual and motivating routine.
