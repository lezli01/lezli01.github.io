Thank you for everyone who downloads and tries our game, we worked very hard to make it. Hope you will enjoy playing it as much as we enjoyed creating it!

Age Of Runes: Genesis is a fantasy themed match-3 puzzle game with RPG elements. Lead your hero through ever hardening stages on five different sets of environments earning rewards and upgrading various skills.

Keywords: match-3, rpg, endless, addictive, fantasy

This is the first ever published game of our indie developer team of two people - Primeval Games - from Hungary.

The game is free for everyone.
