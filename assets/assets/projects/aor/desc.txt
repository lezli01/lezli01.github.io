Age Of Runes: Genesis is a fantasy match-3 puzzle game with RPG elements, built by a two-person indie team from Hungary under the Primeval Games name.

The game blends approachable puzzle mechanics with character progression. Players lead a hero through increasingly challenging stages across five environment sets, earning rewards and upgrading skills along the way.

Highlights:
* Match-3 gameplay with RPG-style progression
* Five themed environment sets
* Reward and skill upgrade systems
* Free-to-play Android release
* First published game from our small indie team

Keywords: match-3, RPG, endless, fantasy, indie game
