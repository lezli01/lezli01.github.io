artinca is an AI-powered real estate price estimator that combines machine learning, live advertising data, market trends and statistics.

The goal was to create a professional, continuously updated housing calculator that helps real estate professionals and private buyers make better-informed decisions.

Core capabilities:
* AI-powered price estimation
* Multiple independently trained models compared during each calculation
* Regular model updates based on current advertisements
* Local advertising trend discovery by municipality
* Historical statistics for prices and listing parameters
* Feedback channel for users and continuous product improvement

artinca was built as a practical decision-support tool: useful for comparing opportunities, understanding market movement and spotting what is common, rare or difficult to find in a given location.
